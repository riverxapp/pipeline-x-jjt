About Us

Our Story

Experience the Taste of Tradition

Founded in 1990, Panda Bistro has been serving exquisite seasonal dishes crafted from family recipes and fresh local ingredients. Our passion for authentic flavors and warm hospitality has made us a beloved dining destination for over three decades.

Our Heritage

We blend tradition with innovation, honoring our roots while embracing new culinary trends. Our chefs are dedicated to creating dishes that tell a story with every bite.

Join us for a memorable dining experience in a cozy, modern setting that feels like home.

Our Mission

At Panda Bistro, we are committed to providing excellent service, fresh ingredients, and creative dishes that delight every guest. Sustainability and community support are at the heart of what we do.

We believe great food brings people together, and we strive to create a welcoming atmosphere for family and friends.
