History & Milestones

From a neighborhood bistro to a destination for modern seasonal dining.

What started as a small dining room with a local following has grown into a restaurant known for elegant simplicity, refined technique, and a kitchen that lets ingredients lead the conversation.

2012 — The first service

Panda Bistro opened with a focused menu, a handful of signature dishes, and a commitment to welcoming every guest like a regular.

2016 — Seasonal menu evolution

The kitchen expanded its farm-to-table relationships, bringing in produce, herbs, and artisanal ingredients at their peak.

2020 — Experience refined

The dining room and service style were reimagined to create a more intimate, polished, and comfortable guest experience.

Today — A modern classic

Panda Bistro continues to evolve while staying true to the same values: quality, hospitality, and memorable flavor.
